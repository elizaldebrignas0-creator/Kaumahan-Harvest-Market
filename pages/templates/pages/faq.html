Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Register as a buyer, browse products from the buyer dashboard, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. All orders are paid via Cash on Delivery (COD).

How can I register as a seller?

On the registration page, choose Seller as your account type, provide your business name, upload a valid business permit, and pin your business location on the map. Your account will be reviewed by an admin before activation.

What payment methods are supported?

Kaumahan currently supports Cash on Delivery (COD) only. Additional payment options may be added in the future.

How does delivery work?

Sellers are responsible for coordinating the delivery of orders to buyers based on the shipping address provided. Order status is updated from Pending to Delivered by the seller once the delivery is completed.

How are ratings and reviews used?

After receiving an order, buyers can rate products from 1 to 5 stars and leave a comment. These reviews help other buyers make informed decisions and encourage quality service from sellers.
